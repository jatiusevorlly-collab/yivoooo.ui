About Us

Welcome to Kim Beauty, a home-based beauty service located in Kg. Lajong, Kudat, Sabah. We specialize in professional hair care services and also offer a range of perfumes and clothing to complement your style. Our journey started by serving close family and friends, and through their support, we continue to grow and expand. At Kim Beauty, we believe in making beauty and self-care more personal, comfortable, and accessible to everyone. Although we are still small and growing, our goal is to improve how we connect with customers and provide a better experience whether through appointments, products, or services. We are passionate about helping you look and feel your best, one step at a time.
